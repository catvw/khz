the lowball

dec ‘24 – jan ‘25
ever wanted to control your cursor with your feet? no? well, now you can!

designing keyboards with integrated cursors, while something that i think should be done by default, is annoying — especially when the keyboard in question is a descendent of the extraordinary cluster keyboard of the ‘90s. so i figured “hey, why don't i just sidestep the entire problem and utilize another limb?” thus the lowball was born. as a friend pointed out.
